Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 9, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: At IR Generator, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and business data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Introduction

IR Generator ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Fervtech. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our invoice and receipt generation platform available at irgenerator.com and through our mobile applications.

By using IR Generator, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use our Service.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use IR Generator, you may provide us with:

Category Data Collected Purpose
Account Information Name, email address, password, company name, phone number Account creation, authentication, communication
Business Data Company details, logo, address, tax information Invoice/receipt generation, customization
Client Information Client names, contact details, addresses Client management, document generation
Transaction Data Invoice details, amounts, payment methods, dates Document creation, record keeping
Payment Information Billing address, payment method details Subscription processing (via third-party processors)

When you use IR Generator, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Session identifiers, preferences, analytics data

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Authentication Providers: Google OAuth (name, email, profile picture)
  • Payment Processors: Paystack, Stripe (transaction status, payment confirmations)
  • Analytics Services: Google Analytics (aggregated usage patterns)

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you of any such change.

5. Data Security

Security Commitment: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data at rest is encrypted in secure cloud servers
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access limitations
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed and salted
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for suspicious activity

5.2 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of your password
  • Using a strong, unique password
  • Logging out of your account when finished
  • Notifying us immediately of any unauthorized access

Important: No system is completely secure. While we implement strong security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please take precautions to protect your account credentials.

6. Data Retention

6.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and for the period necessary to provide our services.

6.2 Inactive Accounts

If your account is inactive for 24 months, we may delete your data after providing 30 days' notice.

6.3 Account Deletion

Upon account deletion:

  • Data is retained for 30 days (configurable in settings: 30, 60, 90 days, or immediate)
  • You can export your data before deletion
  • After the retention period, data is permanently deleted
  • Some data may be retained longer for legal or security purposes

6.4 Backup Data

Backup copies may persist in our systems for up to 90 days after deletion.
