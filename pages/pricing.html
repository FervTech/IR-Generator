Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades are immediate, downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), credit/debit cards, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Is there a free trial?

The Basic plan is free forever! Business plan comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely! Cancel anytime with no questions asked. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. Just contact support and we'll process your refund.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation. You can export all your invoices before canceling.
